Professor mode · IRC and thermochemistry

An IRC is defined in mass-weighted coordinates and follows local steepest descent from the saddle. The harmonic ZPE correction excludes the imaginary mode and does not include thermal, entropic, anharmonic, tunneling, solvent, or dynamical recrossing effects.
